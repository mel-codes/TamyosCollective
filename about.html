ABOUT TAMYOS COLLECTIVE

No fast fashion. No fakes. Just carefully sourced vintage and streetwear finds for those who know what they're looking for.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2023 by Justin Tamayo, TAMYOS COLLECTIVE was created for the community. The idea was simple: help people find pieces that fit their vibe whether that's a cozy hoodie for a study day at a cafe or a standout leather jacket for a night out.

Since then, the Collective has grown to over 700 sales and has hosted multiple pop-up events across Vancouver. At its core, TAMYOS COLLECTIVE is all about bringing people together through style. Keeping you fly, on the go.
